Creating Community.

We invest in people, not buildings. Where you live matters.

DJM Investments is a multi-family investment and management firm with an unmatched enthusiasm, and desire to cultivate community for families throughout Indiana. In 2015, DJM Investments bought its first community in Berne, Indiana and has since expanded to other regions of the state.



At DJM, we invest in people, not buildings. We believe in quality community and quality rental property management- where you're more than a building number and valued as a neighbor.



DJM Investments is staffed with a team dedicated to building community among residents. Through investment in people and properties, DJM encourages positive treatment of residents and apartments alike.
